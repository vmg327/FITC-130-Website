Waxing

Avoid shaving 2 - 4 weeks before waxing because the hair needs to be 1/4 of an inch (length of a grain of rice) for optimal waxing results. Avoid excessive sun exposure 48 hours before your appointment. Please don't apply anything to the skin before waxing (e.g. deodorant, lotion, etc). Avoid exfoliating your skin 2 days before your appointment to avoid skin sensitivity. Book appointments 48 hours before special occasions in case of adverse reactions. Avoid makeup application for 24 hours after waxing. After waxing avoid excessively hot showers, baths, saunas or steam rooms for 24 - 48 hours. Don't suntan for 24 hours after the service. Wear loose clothing so your skin doesn't become irritated. Avoid exfoliating for 24 hours after your appointment. Don't swim in lakes or oceans after your appointment because this can cause skin rashes and reactions. Furthermore, don't workout for 24 hours after because sweat can infect open follicles. Please don't apply any scented products on the waxed area for 24 hours.
