Products are handmade without severe heating or hydrogenating processes that can affect the nutritional content and potency of natural ingredients. Free of parabens, animal by-products, propylene glycol, sodium lauryl sulfates, harmful colorants and fragrances, mineral oils, petroleum, and other harsh cosmetic chemicals.
